# Module 04: Accounts and Transactions

## What You'll Learn

After Module 03 (consensus), you understand **how blocks are created**. This module covers **what's in those blocks**: accounts and transactions.

**Key questions:**
- How are accounts represented?
- How do transactions work under the hood?
- Why do I need to pay gas?
- What are nonces and why do they matter?
